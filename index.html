Madrasa Suffatul Hasanain is a distinguished Islamic educational institution located in the heart of Madanapalle, Andhra Pradesh. We are dedicated to nurturing young minds through authentic Islamic scholarship combined with contemporary education.

Founded with the noble vision of preserving and propagating the knowledge of the Quran and Sunnah, our madrasa provides a structured, compassionate, and intellectually rich environment for students of all ages.

Our institution follows a comprehensive curriculum rooted in traditional Islamic sciences, including Hifz, Tajweed, Fiqh, Hadith, and Arabic language, ensuring students grow into knowledgeable, principled, and contributing members of society.

Our Programs & Courses

📚
ناظرة
Nazira (Quran Reading)

Foundational Quran reading program for beginners, ensuring correct pronunciation and fluency before advancing to higher studies.

Ages 9+ 6 Months Morning Batch
📗
حفظ القرآن
Hifz-ul-Quran (Memorization)

A comprehensive program for complete memorization of the Holy Quran with proper Tajweed, guided by certified Huffaz under a structured routine.

Ages 9+ 3–5 Years Full-time
🎓
دورة عالم
5-Year Aalim Course

A complete and intensive Islamic scholarship program covering Tafseer, Hadith, Fiqh, Usool, Arabic grammar, and Islamic history. Graduates are awarded the title of Aalim and are fully equipped to serve as scholars, Imams, and community leaders.

Ages 9+ 5 Years Full-time Certificate Awarded
🎙️
تجويد
Tajweed & Qira'at

Master the rules of Quranic recitation with detailed study of articulation points, elongation, and the science of Tajweed.

Ages 9+ 1–2 Years Part-time Available
🌟
اللغة العربية
Arabic Language

Structured Arabic language courses from beginner to advanced, covering grammar, vocabulary, reading, writing, and conversation.

Ages 9+ 1 Year Flexible Timing
🕌
عقيدة
Aqeedah & Islamic Studies

Foundational courses in Islamic theology, beliefs, Seerah of the Prophet ﷺ, and the history of Islam for young students.

Ages 9+ Ongoing Weekend Classes
